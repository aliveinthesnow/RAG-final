Hostels and Halls of Residence (NIT Warangal)\n\n## About\nHalls of Residence\nNIT Warangal is a fully residential campus and provides accommodation facilities to all the students admitted to various courses. The Institute has thirteen halls of Residence for boys and three for girls. The halls of Residence administration is managed by the Chief Warden along with the team of Wardens and Assistant Wardens. The Warden is the administrative head of each hall of Residence who will be assisted by Assistant Wardens. The Wardens will form the halls committee comprising the students who will act as Maintenance Secretary, Environment Secretary, Cultural Secretary and Games & Sports Secretary. The committee decides the pattern and the pace of life of each Hall.\nThe Halls of Residence Advisory Council is the apex decision making body concerning the Halls of Residence which is headed by the Director and is responsible for major policy decisions of the Hall of Residence administration.\nThe Hall Disciplinary Committee (HDC) will look after any act of indiscipline in a Hall of residence and will be investigated by a Hall Disciplinary Committee which will recommend the action to the Warden of the Hall. He /she will decide the course of action to be taken and implement it. However, in case the matter is of a serious nature, the warden will forward it to the Dean (SW) through the Chief Warden. The Dean (SW) who is the Chairman of the ISDC will take necessary action.\nSpecial facilities available in halls of residence for students with disabilities\nRamps in all halls of residence\nEasy access toilets for boarders with disabilities.\nIn halls of Residence, rooms are reserved for physically challenged boarders. These rooms are attached to the toilet and bathroom. These rooms are kept near the main entrance of the hall for easy access by the physically challenged boarders.\n24 x 7 support is being provided. In certain cases, room service is also available such as getting meals in time at their doorstep.\nInstitute Food Courts\nThere are five Institute Food Courts which are running on a contract basis known as \u201cIFC A, IFC B, IFC C, IFC D, and Priyadarshini mess\u201d for both boy's and girl\u2019s halls of residence premises. There are a variety of menus for accommodating the diversity of students which Includes North Indian, South Indian, and mixed Veg menu and Non- veg varieties. Each mess has a Professor In charge of the mess who will look after the administration of the mess. There are mess representatives elected among the students who will look after the day-to-day functioning of the mess under the supervision of the Professor In charge.\nNote: For any issue related to the halls of residence ( Housekeeping, electrical, carpentry, plumbing etc.,) the students are advised to contact concerned and not to the Director, Dean (SW) and Chief warden directly.
